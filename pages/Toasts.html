Toasts

Toasts in Bootstrap are small notifications that appear at the bottom right corner of the screen. They're used to display temporary messages or alerts and can be customized in terms of appearance, behavior, and duration using CSS and JavaScript. They're a useful and flexible tool for improving the user experience.
