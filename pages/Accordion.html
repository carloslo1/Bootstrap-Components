Accordion

The accordion component in Bootstrap is a UI element that organizes content into expandable and collapsible panels. It is useful for managing large amounts of content or limited screen space. Accordions consist of a container with multiple items, each with a header and a content area. Headers act as triggers for expanding or collapsing content areas, enabling users to focus on one section at a time.

Flush

The flush accordion has no borders or rounded corners around its panels and can be used to create a seamless look between adjacent panels.
