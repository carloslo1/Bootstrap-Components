Popovers

Popovers in Bootstrap are small overlays that provide additional information or context to users. They can be triggered by clicking, hovering, or focusing on elements and can contain text, images, or forms.

Live Demo

The live demo showcases the functionality and customization options of popovers, including triggers, placement, and content.

Disabled Elements

Disabled elements are those that are non-clickable and have reduced opacity, but can still display content via a popover.
